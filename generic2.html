Why did I choose RMIT?


There are 3 main reasons why I choose to study at RMIT. First of all, I really love the international environment of this school because I can improve my English skills, it makes me feel more confident when I communicate with foreign people. Moreover, maybe I can learn more and more languages, which is very helpful for me. For example, I will know who is cursing me though they do not use English. Secondly, RMIT Vietnam tuition fee is cheaper than studying abroad but I still have the same knowledge as RMIT Australia. Finally, studying at RMIT, I will have more time to improve my practical skills by doing group assignments and totally focus on my major because I do not have to learn unnecessary subjects like philosophy like studying at other schools.
