What is my interest in IT?


Nearly 10 years ago, I have had a huge love with Artificial Intelligent after I watched Wall - E on Disney Channel. That movie shows us about human lives in the future when the robots become advanced which is possible to replace people in every single work of life. When I was 8 or 9 years old, I have thought that it is very cool if I could make one like that for myself so I can enjoy my life without doing anything. Although there are some changes in my thoughts about this idea, l excited about this kind of subject because I am quite messy so I want a robot which can help to clean my room. Therefore, I started to learn Pascal in secondary school and engaged in some robotic competitions in highschool like Robotacon and SRobot. Furthermore, I also participated in IT Bootcamp at SaigonTech college to learn about IoT and I have made a balanced robot with my team.
